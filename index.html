Who We Are

Setting & Nurturing Standards in Aviation

Our Mission

To offer world-class flight training, set and nurture standards in aviation across East Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

To offer world-class flight training that produces competent, confident pilots ready for the global aviation industry.

Safety First

Safety of our pilots and aircraft is our top priority. Our professional, licensed aircraft engineers ensure all aircraft are serviceable at all times.

Why Standards Aviation?

We take pride in providing top-notch pilot training where we make our students — whether career-oriented or flying for fun — feel well taken care of. We are keen on providing standard training in a serene and tranquil environment.

  • ✓ One-on-one student to instructor training
  • ✓ Instructors with general aviation, airline & military experience
  • ✓ Structured ground school & flight training integration
  • ✓ Training delivered within stipulated timelines
  • ✓ Graduate exposure matching world-class airline demands
What We Offer

Training Programmes & Fee Structure

We offer structured training integrating ground school and flight training programmes. Our instructors have extensive experience ranging from general aviation, airline, and local military.
